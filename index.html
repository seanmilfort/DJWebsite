About Me

Always bringing the good vibes!

Seanzie is a DJ from the Central Jersey/Philadelphia area! He started to DJ during his undergrad in college and has been doing it ever since! While he does specialize in the club/bar scene, he has a versatile knowledge base of music that is perfect for any occasion. You can expect a high energy and great time with Seanzie. For him, DJing is all about having a good time and bringing the energy for everyone else. When he is not being a DJ, Seanzie is working in IT and also working as a developer.
